Sloppy is free, under the GNU Public License, for you to use and modify.

If you can speak a language (in addition to English) fluently, you can contribute by helping to translate Sloppy. If you can code, you can contribute by sending in bug fixes or enhancements. Please do get in touch and check out the translation notes at http://code.google.com/p/sloppy/wiki/ForTranslators.
